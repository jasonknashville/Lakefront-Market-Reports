What Does Lakefront Actually Look Like?
Key observations for buyers exploring Old Hickory Lake

🎯 The Budget Is In Play

In 2025, the median lakefront sale was $1.229M. That means roughly half of all closings fell below that number. Sub-$1.5M buyers are competing for real inventory — not the bottom of the barrel.

⚠️ The Shrinking Window

In 2019, 60%+ of lakefront sales fell under $1M. By 2025, the median alone is $1.229M. The entire market has shifted upward. Buyers who wait may find their dollar goes further today than in 2026 or 2027.

✅ City Matters Significantly

Hendersonville and Old Hickory tend to offer the widest range of price points. If $1.5M is your ceiling, targeting Hendersonville's established lake neighborhoods gives you the best odds.

📈 Strong Long-Term Appreciation

At +66% median price growth over six years ($740K → $1.229M), Old Hickory Lake has outpaced many traditional luxury markets. Price-per-SF has grown from $226 to $370 — a strong compounding return.
